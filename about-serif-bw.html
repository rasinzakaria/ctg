Our Story

Who We Are

At CTG Brands, we don't just move products — we bring possibilities to life. Since 1985, we've been importing and distributing innovative consumer packaged goods across North America, earning the trust of thousands of retailers by consistently delivering on-trend, high-quality products.

With over 40 years of experience, our reach now extends across Canada, the United States, the Caribbean, and South America. Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, we stay ahead of what's next so that our partners always have access to products that excite, inspire, and keep customers coming back.

As we continue to grow, CTG Brands has officially launched a dedicated U.S. sales force — an exciting step forward in our expansion into the American market. With experienced professionals on the ground and a rapidly growing team, we're strengthening our presence and expanding our ability to support an even wider range of retailers across North America.

At CTG Brands, we believe growth is built on strong relationships, bold ideas, and the drive to help our partners succeed. And as we continue to evolve, we remain focused on pushing boundaries, uncovering new opportunities, and growing together — every step of the way.

Our History

1985

Canasia Toys & Gifts (CTG) Brands Inc. started as a small investment with three partners. Our first facility was a 3,000 square foot space located in Downsview, Ontario with less than 300 products to offer.

1993

Our Newkirk Warehouse Opening with our star product, the Puppet, where we sold thousands of units quickly within the year.

1998

We visited China during this time to source new items as we started to expand our product lines, adding Seasonal and Home Décor merchandise.

2003

Expanded to a 244,000 square feet multifunctional warehouse and showroom in Vaughan, Ontario.

2015

Richmond Grand Opening for our 131,000 square feet multifunctional warehouse and showroom.

2022

CTG Tradeshow Booth at the 2022 Cangift Market Show.
