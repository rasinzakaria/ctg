Our Story.

CTG Brands is one of North America's leading importers and distributors of consumer packaged goods. From our 244,000 square foot headquarters in Vaughan, Ontario and our 131,000 square foot facility in Richmond, British Columbia, we serve thousands of retailers across Canada and the United States with a portfolio spanning everyday essentials, seasonal products, and home décor.

Four decades in the wholesale industry have taught us that success is built on relationships, not transactions. We work closely with our retail partners at every stage — from sourcing and product development to fulfillment and market strategy — delivering the kind of value that keeps shelves stocked and customers coming back.

Consumers are rarely driven by price alone. The focus at CTG is on value — and that's where the company excels. — CTG Brands Leadership

40 Years of Growth.

1985

Canasia Toys & Gifts (CTG) Brands Inc. started as a small investment with three partners. Our first facility was a 3,000 square foot space in Downsview, Ontario with less than 300 products to offer.

1993

Newkirk warehouse opening with our star product, the Puppet, where we sold thousands of units quickly within the year.

1998

We visited China to source new items as we started to expand our product lines, adding Seasonal and Home Décor merchandise.

2003

Expanded to a 244,000 square foot multifunctional warehouse and showroom in Vaughan, Ontario.

2005

CTG Brands proudly celebrates its 25th anniversary.

2015

Richmond, Vancouver grand opening for our 131,000 square foot multifunctional warehouse and showroom.

2020

We acquired AZ Trading Co.'s home décor business and related assets.

2023

Malinda Distributors joined CTG Brands, marking our expansion into the de-alcoholized wine category.

2024

CTG Brands expanded beyond Canada, actively developing our presence in the United States market.

2025

CTG Brands celebrates its 40th anniversary.

2025

CTG developed a strategic partnership with Kitchen Stuff Plus, granting exclusive U.S. distribution rights for the Canadian retailer's branded products.

2025

CTG completed the strategic acquisition of GiftCraft assets, accelerating CTG Brands' expansion across North America.

Growth is built on strong relationships, bold ideas, and the drive to help our partners succeed.
