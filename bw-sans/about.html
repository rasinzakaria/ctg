Our Story

Who We Are

At CTG Brands, we don't just move products — we bring possibilities to life. Since 1985, we've been importing and distributing innovative consumer packaged goods across North America, earning the trust of thousands of retailers by consistently delivering on-trend, high-quality products.

With over 40 years of experience, our reach now extends across Canada, the United States, the Caribbean, and South America. Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, we stay ahead of what's next so that our partners always have access to products that excite, inspire, and keep customers coming back.

Consumers are rarely driven by price alone. The focus at CTG is on value — and that's where the company excels.

We bring years of wholesale expertise together with a hands-on, partner-first approach. From sourcing to fulfillment, we deliver clarity, speed, and market insight at every stage — helping our partners grow, season after season.

Since 1985

Our Journey

In 1985, Canasia Toys & Gifts (CTG) Brands Inc. started as a small investment with three partners. Our first facility was a 3,000 square foot space in Downsview, Ontario with less than 300 products to offer. By 1993, we had opened our Newkirk warehouse with our star product — the Puppet — selling thousands of units within the year.

In 1998, we made our first trip to China to source new items, expanding our product lines to include Seasonal and Home Décor merchandise. This was a pivotal moment — the beginning of CTG's transformation into a full-spectrum consumer goods distributor.

By 2003, we expanded to a 244,000 square foot multifunctional warehouse and showroom in Vaughan, Ontario. And in 2015, we celebrated the grand opening of our 131,000 square foot Richmond facility — marking a new chapter of coast-to-coast coverage across Canada.

As we continue to grow, CTG Brands has officially launched a dedicated U.S. sales force — an exciting step forward in our expansion into the American market. And as we continue to evolve, we remain focused on pushing boundaries, uncovering new opportunities, and growing together — every step of the way.
